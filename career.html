Paul McCartney: Career

Short Introduction

Sir James Paul McCartney is an English singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, and composer. He gained worldwide fame as the bass guitarist and singer for the Beatles. He is the most famous artist of all time.

List of Skills/Talents

  • Bass Guitar
  • Acoustic Guitar
  • Electric Guitar
  • Vocals
  • Keyboards
  • Drums

List of Awards

  • NME Awards (1963, 1964, 1965, 1966, 1968, 1970, 1971, 1974, 1976, 1980, 2000, 2005, 2014)
  • Classical Brit Awards (1977, 1983, 2007, 2008)
  • Academy Awards (1971)
  • MTV Video Music Awards (1984)
  • Grammy Awards (1965, 1967, 1968, 1971, 1972, 1975, 1980, 1997, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2015)
